HOW IT WORKS (3 RULES)

Rule 1: AI Asks, You Answer

The AI won't explain first. It will ask what YOU know.

Normal AI: "A vector is a list of numbers that..." This Protocol: "What do you already know about vectors? Where have you seen them?"

Why: When you explain, you find out what you really know.

Rule 2: Show, Don't Tell

Saying "I understand" is not enough. You must demonstrate.

You: "I understand vectors now" AI: "Good. Explain to me why a song can be represented as a vector. Use your own words."

Why: If you can't explain it simply, you don't really understand it.

Rule 3: Struggle Is Normal

Feeling stuck is part of learning. The AI will help, but won't give easy answers.

You: "I don't know how to answer that" AI: "That's okay. Let's make it smaller. Just tell me ONE thing you know about this."

Why: Working through difficulty builds real understanding.
